# Job Description: Junior AI Engineer — TechVest

**Company:** TechVest Financial Technology  
**Role:** Junior AI Engineer  
**Level:** Entry-Level / Junior (0–2 years experience)  
**Team:** AI Products & Research  

---

## About the Role

TechVest is building the next generation of AI-powered financial tools. We are looking for a Junior AI Engineer who can contribute to real ML/LLM projects from day one. You will work closely with senior engineers, run experiments, build pipelines, and communicate your findings clearly.

---

## Responsibilities

- Build and iterate on ML models (regression, classification, NLP) for production use cases.
- Integrate large language model APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) into product workflows.
- Write clean, testable Python code with proper documentation.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (product, data, backend) and present results clearly.
- Maintain experiment tracking, model versioning, and basic MLOps hygiene.

---

## Requirements

1. **Python proficiency** — comfortable writing production-quality Python; familiarity with data science stack (NumPy, pandas).
2. **ML fundamentals** — understands regression, classification, basic deep learning (backprop, CNNs/RNNs or Transformers).
3. **At least one ML/LLM framework** — scikit-learn, PyTorch, TensorFlow, LangChain, or equivalent; real project experience preferred.
4. **Hands-on project evidence** — demonstrable projects (GitHub, Kaggle, internships, coursework with measurable outcomes); coursework-only is borderline.
5. **Communication ability** — can write clear documentation, present work to non-technical stakeholders, and collaborate on a team.
6. **0–2 years experience** — junior role; we value project depth over tenure.

---

## Nice to Have

- Experience with vector databases (Pinecone, Chroma, FAISS).
- Familiarity with MLflow, Weights & Biases, or similar experiment tracking.
- Basic cloud exposure (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI).

---

## What We Offer

- Mentorship from senior AI engineers.
- Access to GPU compute for experiments.
- Flexible hybrid work policy.
